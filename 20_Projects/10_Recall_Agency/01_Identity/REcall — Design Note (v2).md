Recall Website Design Note

## REcall — Design Note (v2)

## Executive Summary

REcall is positioned as a premium, tech-driven platform at the intersection of IT services, cybersecurity, and PropTech. The new website must reflect **authority, precision, and innovation**, while remaining frictionless for conversion.

Core goals:

- Establish trust instantly (cybersecurity + real estate credibility)
- Clearly communicate bundled IT + Real Estate value
- Drive high-intent leads (B2B, investors, property owners)
- Showcase technological edge (AI, OSINT, automation)

Design philosophy:

- Minimalist but powerful
- Dark-tech aesthetic with sharp contrasts
- Data-first storytelling
- Conversion-focused structure

---

## Brand Positioning

### Core Identity

- Cybersecurity + PropTech hybrid
- High-end, strategic, not commodity services
- “Infrastructure layer” for modern real estate

### Tone

- Confident, precise, no fluff
- Results-driven
- Slightly provocative (challenge outdated real estate practices)

### Visual Direction

- Dark mode dominant (black / deep charcoal)
- Accent colors: electric blue / neon green (tech signal)
- Typography: modern sans-serif (clean, sharp)

---

## UX/UI Principles

### 1\. Clarity over creativity

Users must understand within 5 seconds:

- What REcall does
- Who it’s for
- Why it’s different

### 2\. Conversion-first architecture

Every section must push toward:

- Contact
- Demo
- Consultation

### 3\. Modular structure

Reusable blocks for:

- Services
- Case studies
- Tech stack

### 4\. Perceived performance

- Fast loading
- Smooth micro-interactions
- Subtle animations (no gimmicks)
