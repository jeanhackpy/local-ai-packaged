## Maintenance Procedures and Schedules

This document outlines system maintenance procedures and schedules.

## Regular Maintenance Tasks

- Weekly system updates (Saturday morning)
- Monthly backup verification (see [[Development/VPS Hostinger & WordPress#Automated Backups]], [[Infra/VPS_Hostinger/VPS_Exploration_2026-01-27#Snapshots & Backups]])
- Quarterly security audit (see [[SystemPolicy/03_Security]])
- Annual hardware inspection

## Backup Strategies

- Daily automated backups to cloud storage
- Weekly full system snapshots
- Monthly offsite backups
- Yearly archive of important data

## Monitoring and Logging

- Daily log review for anomalies (see [[Infra/VPS_Hostinger/Plan_Action_Monitoring#1-Script-de-monitoring-Python]])
- Weekly performance metrics analysis (see [[Infra/VPS_Hostinger/VPS_Exploration_2026-01-27#Métriques sur 24h]])
- Monthly capacity planning
- Annual infrastructure review

## Recovery Procedures

- Documented disaster recovery plan
- Regular recovery testing
- Emergency contact procedures
- Backup restoration workflows
