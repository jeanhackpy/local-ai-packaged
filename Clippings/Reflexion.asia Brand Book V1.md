Reflexion.asia Brand Book V1

## Reflexion.asia – Brand Book v1.0

*Last updated: 8 Jul 2025*

---

## 0 · Purpose & Positioning

**Mission** Empower global property dreamers to invest and live in Thailand with radical transparency, data‑driven insight, and concierge‑level service.

**Vision** Re‑imagine real‑estate discovery in Southeast Asia, fusing local expertise, immersive tech, and a human touch.

**Positioning Statement** *For French‑speaking investors and digital nomads seeking Thai property, Reflexion.asia is the luxury‑minded, tech‑forward agency that bridges cultures through data‑rich storytelling and white‑glove guidance — unlike generic listing portals, we curate, predict, and personally accompany every client journey.*

---

## 1 · Brand DNA

| Pillar | Description | Proof Points |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Explorer** | Adventurous, culturally curious, boundary‑pushing | Bilingual team, cross‑disciplinary founders |
| **Sage** | Data‑driven, transparent, educational | OSINT‑based market reports, AI‑powered matching |
| **Host** | Warm, service‑obsessed, trustworthy | Concierge onboarding, magic‑link dashboards |

**Core Values** Curiosity · Trust · Precision · Hospitality · Sustainability

**Personality** *Confident mentor* meets *tech futurist*. Speak with clarity, challenge conventions, respect local nuance.

---

## 2 · Audience Profiles

1. **Primary — French HNW & UHNW buyers (35‑55)** *Pain*: Information opacity, language barrier. *Need*: Reliable, on‑the‑ground advisor.
2. **Secondary — Digital‑nomad entrepreneurs (25‑40)** *Pain*: Time‑poor, want turnkey solutions. *Need*: Fast, mobile‑first experience.
3. **Tertiary — Thai developers & partners** *Pain*: Access to European market. *Need*: Credible channel partner.

---

## 3 · Verbal Identity

### 3.1 Tone of Voice Principles

| Principle | Do | Don’t |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Visionary** | Paint possibilities, reference future trends | Over‑promise, use empty buzzwords |
| **Candid** | Give numbers, cite sources | Hide risks, waffle |
| **Conversational Luxury** | Use sensory language, short sentences | Sound aloof or jargon‑heavy |
| **Cross‑Cultural** | Provide French + English snippets, note Thai context | Assume single‑culture worldview |

### 3.2 Signature Vocabulary

- *Immersive viewing*, *smart due‑diligence*, *Thai hospitality*, *investment serenity*.
- Replace “cheap/bargain” ➜ “value‑rich”.
- Use “residence” over “unit”; “sanctuary” over “apartment” when describing high‑end.

### 3.3 Messaging Hierarchy

1. **Hook** Dream‑driven aspiration (sunrise over Andaman).
2. **Proof** Data snapshot (yield, absorption rate).
3. **Guide** Call‑to‑action (book a virtual tour).

### 3.4 Bilingual Rules

- Headlines in **English**, sub‑headlines in *French italics* when both appear.
- Keep sentence length ≤ 20 words in English, ≤ 25 in French.

## 4 · Visual Identity

### 4.1 Logo System

- **Primary Mark** Monoline “R” formed by Thai sala roof silhouette.
- **Safe‑Zone** ≥ ½ the height of the “R” on all sides.
- **Min Size** 24 px height digital, 8 mm print.
- **Variants** Full‑lockup (R + wordmark), Symbol‑only, Horizontal.
- **Don’ts** No drop‑shadows, no gradients, don’t rotate.

### 4.2 Color Palette

| Role | HEX | RGB | CMYK |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Sapphire Midnight (Primary)** | #002D55 | 0 45 85 | 100 50 15 54 |
| **Emerald Lagoon (Accent)** | #007C71 | 0 124 113 | 93 0 34 51 |
| **Champagne Gold (Highlight)** | #CBA24A | 203 162 74 | 18 29 82 7 |
| **Silk Ivory (Background)** | #F5F6F4 | 245 246 244 | 2 0 2 0 |
| **Graphite Shadow (Text)** | #222222 | 34 34 34 | 0 0 0 87 |

Accessibility: Maintain ≥ 4.5:1 contrast for body text on backgrounds.

### 4.3 Typography

| Usage | Font | Fallback | Sample |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Display** (H1–H3) | *Playfair Display* (Serif) | Georgia | "Phuket Panorama" |
| **Body** (P, UI) | *Inter* | Arial | "Seamless expat living" |
| **Numeric/Code** | *Space Grotesk* | monospace | 7.2 % net yield |

Hierarchy (desktop): H1 48 px · H2 36 px · H3 24 px · Body 18 px · Caption 14 px.

### 4.4 Photography & Imagery

- **Mood** Golden‑hour, natural light, candid human element.
- **Composition** Rule of thirds, wide negative space for overlay copy.
- **Color Grading** Warm highlights, slight teal shadows, uniform LUT.
- **Don’ts** Over‑HDR, obvious stock photos, cluttered interiors.

### 4.5 Illustration & Iconography

- Thin‑line 2 px stroke, 24 px grid. Rounded terminals echo “R” curves.
- Minimal fills; use Emerald Lagoon for active states.

### 4.6 Motion & Interaction

- Duration: 250–400 ms ease‑out.
- Parallax on hero image capped at 15 % movement.
- Scroll‑triggered fade‑up for property cards.

---

## 5 · Layout & Components

- **Grid** 12‑column flex (90 % width, 5 % gutters). Mobile 4‑column.
- **Spacing** Base unit 8 px. Sections 5 × base.
- **Buttons** Pill radius 24 px, height 48 px. Primary in Emerald, hover ➜ Sapphire fill with Gold text.
- **Cards** Soft shadow 0 2 8 rgba(0,0,0,.06), 16 px radius.
- **Forms** Floating labels, error state in Gold #CBA24A at 80 % opacity.

## 7 · Social‑Media Voice & Etiquette

- Emojis: ✅✈️🏝 used sparingly (max 2 per post).
- Hashtags: #InvestInThailand #BangkokCondos #ReflexionAsia (limit 5).
- Reply speed target: < 1 h during 9‑18 h ICT.

---

## 8 · Accessibility & Inclusivity

- WCAG 2.2 AA minimum.
- Provide alt text ≤ 140 chars, bilingual when contextually relevant.
- Never use colour‑alone to convey meaning; add icon or label.

## 9 · Governance & Maintenance
