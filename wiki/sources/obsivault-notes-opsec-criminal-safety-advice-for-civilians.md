# OpSec - Criminal Safety Advice for Civilians

## Summary

Operational Security guide adapted for everyday citizens based on criminal tradecraft. Core advice: minimize digital footprint, use strong passwords (15-20 chars), cover webcams, use mic locks. Mobile: downgrade to non-smartphone or jailbroken Android with GrapheneOS. PC: remove bloatware, use VeraCrypt for encryption, Malwarebytes for protection. VPN: MullvadVPN only (with Monero payment). Browser: Mullvad Browser or Brave with PrivacyBadger, UBlockOrigin, NoScript. Email: MailFence over ProtonMail. Car data collection warning. Dog (German Shepherd/Doberman) for physical security. Key concept: ADINT (Ad Intelligence) as threat alongside OSINT.
