# Dynamic Map-Based Data-Centric Tourism Digital Twins

## Summary

ArXiv paper (2603.00079) proposing a QGIS-based, data-centric approach to digital twin frameworks for managing tourism and cultural heritage in smart cities. Validated through Salvador Historic Center (Pelourinho) case study facing overtourism challenges.

## Key Concepts

- **QGIS digital twin** platform for heritage tourism management
- **Data silos integration** across tourism stakeholders
- **Overtourism control** mechanisms and carrying capacity
- **Heritage preservation** via data-driven urban resilience
- **Climate adaptation** measures for cultural assets
