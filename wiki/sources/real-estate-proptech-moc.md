## Real Estate & Proptech MOC

**Source:** OSINT vault index

### Overview

This is a Map of Content (MOC) serving as the master index for Real Estate & PropTech resources. It uses Dataview queries to list content from subdirectories.

### Structure

The MOC references three main categories:

1. **Market Analysis** — `30_Ressources/RealEstate/MarketAnalysis`
2. **Investment** — `30_Ressources/RealEstate/Investment`
3. **Concepts** — `30_Ressources/RealEstate/Concepts`

### Note

This MOC uses Obsidian Dataview plugin for dynamic listing. When imported into SystemMac/WIKI, the Dataview queries won't execute but the structure is preserved for reference.
