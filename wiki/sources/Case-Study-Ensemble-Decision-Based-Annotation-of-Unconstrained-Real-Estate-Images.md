# Case Study: Ensemble Decision-Based Annotation of Unconstrained Real Estate Images

## Abstract

This paper describes a proof-of-concept for annotating real estate images using simple iterative rule-based semi-supervised learning. The study gained important insights into content characteristics and uniqueness of individual image classes as well as essential requirements for practical implementation.
