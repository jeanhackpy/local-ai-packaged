# Analyze Patent - Patent Examination Framework

## Summary

A patent examiner framework for evaluating patent applications. Analyzes field of technology, problem, solution, advantages over state of art, determines novelty (not part of prior art) and inventive step (not obvious to skilled artisan), provides 1000-character summary and up to 20 keywords.

## Key Concepts

- **Field**: Technology domain identification
- **Problem**: Issue addressed by the patent
- **Solution**: Detailed explanation with all steps
- **Advantage**: Benefits over known state of art
- **Novelty**: Not part of state of art (written, oral, use, or other)
- **Inventive Step**: Not obvious to person skilled in art
- **Output**: Verbose, no space limitations, bulleted lists

## Connections

- Used in technology intelligence workflows
- Complements paper analysis for evaluating innovations
- Related to threat modeling for understanding competitive landscape
