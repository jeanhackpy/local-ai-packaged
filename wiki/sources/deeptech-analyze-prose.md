# Analyze Prose - Writing Quality Evaluation

## Summary

An expert writer/editor framework for evaluating writing quality across three dimensions: NOVELTY (A-F rating for how surprising/fresh ideas are), CLARITY (Crystal to Chaotic scale), and PROSE (Inspired to Weak). Provides bulleted improvement recommendations and an overall grade that cannot exceed the lowest rating.

## Key Concepts

- **Novelty ratings**: A (Novel) >90%, B (Fresh) 80-90%, C (Incremental) 50-80%, D (Derivative) 20-50%, F (Stale) <20%
- **Clarity scale**: A (Crystal), B (Clean), C (Kludgy), D (Confusing), F (Chaotic)
- **Prose scale**: A (Inspired), B (Distinctive), C (Standard), D (Stale), F (Weak)
- **Overall rating**: Minimum of the three dimension ratings
- **Recommendations**: No more than 16 words per bullet
