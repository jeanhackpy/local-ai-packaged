## Résumé

Real-time streaming RAG from GPU-accelerated SDR (software-defined radio). Captures RF signals, transcribes speech, embeds and indexes in real-time. Temporal RAG queries ("summarize last 5 minutes"). Sub-5-second end-to-end latency.

## Points clés

- GPU-accelerated SDR captures and processes RF signals in real-time
- Speech transcription, embedding, and indexing pipeline with temporal query support
- Sub-5-second end-to-end latency for speech-to-searchable-insight pipeline
