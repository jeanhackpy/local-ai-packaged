# Ensemble Decision-Based Annotation of Real Estate Images

## Summary

ArXiv paper (2309.15097) demonstrating a proof-of-concept for annotating real estate images using iterative rule-based semi-supervised learning. The study extracts insights into content characteristics and uniqueness of image classes, plus essential requirements for practical implementation.

## Key Concepts

- **Semi-supervised learning** for image annotation at scale
- **Ensemble decision** combining multiple classifiers
- **Real estate image classification** (interior/exterior, room types, quality)
- **Content-based image retrieval** for property matching
- Iterative annotation pipeline reducing manual labeling burden
