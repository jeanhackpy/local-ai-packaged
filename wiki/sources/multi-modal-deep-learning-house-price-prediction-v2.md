# Multi-Modal Deep Learning House Price Prediction

## Summary

ArXiv paper (2024) on house price prediction using multi-modal approach combining raw attributes, text descriptions, images, and neighborhood geo-spatial data. Learns joint embeddings across modalities for improved price prediction accuracy.

## Key Concepts

- **Joint embedding** across visual, textual, and structured data
- **Geo-spatial features** for neighborhood context
- **Text embeddings** from property descriptions
- **Image features** from property photos
- Improved accuracy vs. numerical-only baselines
