# Psychology Study Guides

## Summary

Comprehensive psychology glossary covering major theories and concepts. Topics include: Piaget's stages (sensorimotor, preoperational, concrete operational, formal operational), attachment theory, attribution theory, classical/operant conditioning, cognitive biases, Maslow's hierarchy, Kohlberg's moral development, locus of control, metacognition, schema theory, social penetration theory, and sublimation. Each entry provides definitions and illustrative examples for educational purposes.

## Key Concepts

- Piaget cognitive development stages
- Attachment styles and relationship formation
- Conditioning: classical vs operant reinforcement
- Cognitive biases as mental shortcuts/heuristics
- Maslow: self-actualization as fulfillment of potential
- Schema: mental frameworks organizing information
- Moral development stages (Kohlberg)
- Intrinsic vs extrinsic motivation
