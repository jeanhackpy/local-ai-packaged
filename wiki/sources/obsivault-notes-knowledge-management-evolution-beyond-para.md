# Knowledge Management Evolution - Beyond PARA to Collective Intelligence

## Summary

Article exploring evolution of knowledge management from PARA system toward collective intelligence. Traces knowledge tools from paper/pencil through whiteboards (2D) to digital gardens. Introduces Visuospatial Working Memory (VSWM) concept - integration of reason, logic, and visual memory. Argues for multi-disciplinary approach to knowledge tools, applying concepts from one field to another. Notes shift in how technology relates to thinking and learning - toward natural brain processes.

## Key Concepts

- PARA: Projects, Areas, Resources, Archives
- Collective intelligence as knowledge management objective
- Visuospatial Working Memory (VSWM)
- Paper → Whiteboard → Digital Garden evolution
- Multi-disciplinarian cross-pollination
- Knowledge as power, data as currency
