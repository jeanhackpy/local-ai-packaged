## Résumé

NVIDIA blueprint for GenAI catalog enrichment. Nemotron VLM analyzes product images, FLUX Kontext generates localized variations, TRELLIS creates 3D GLB assets. 10 regional locales. VLM quality scoring. Automated quality assessment.

## Points clés

- Nemotron VLM analyzes product images; FLUX Kontext generates localized variations per region
- TRELLIS creates 3D GLB assets for immersive product visualization
- Supports 10 regional locales with automated VLM quality scoring and assessment
