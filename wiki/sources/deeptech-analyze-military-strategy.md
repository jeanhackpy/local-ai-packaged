# Analyze Military Strategy - Battle Analysis Framework

## Summary

A military historian and strategic analyst framework for dissecting historical battles. Produces 50-word overview, commander lists, 10-20 strategic decisions (20 words each), 15-30 strengths/weaknesses per side, 10-20 pivotal moments (16 words), tactical comparison, logistical factors, and 100-150 word consequences analysis.

## Key Concepts

- **Battle Overview**: 50 words max with date, location, combatants
- **Commanders**: Primary commanders for each side
- **Strategic Decisions**: 10-20 items, exactly 20 words each
- **Pivotal Moments**: 10-20 items, exactly 16 words each
- **Logistical Factors**: 10-20 items, exactly 16 words each
- **Output format**: Two-column for strengths/weaknesses and tactical comparison

## Connections

- Applied in OSINT threat analysis workflows
- Used for historical pattern analysis in strategic planning
- Complements analyze_claims for evaluating strategic arguments
