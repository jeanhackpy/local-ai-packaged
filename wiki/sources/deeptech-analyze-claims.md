# Analyze Claims - Truth Claim Evaluation

## Summary

An objectivity-focused analyzer for evaluating truth claims and arguments. For each claim, provides evidence in support, counter-evidence, identifies logical fallacies, rates claim quality (A-F scale), and provides characterization labels (specious, emotional, progressive, conservative, etc.).

## Key Concepts

- **Output sections**: Argument Summary, Truth Claims (with claim support, refutation, fallacies, rating, labels), Overall Score (lowest/highest/average), Overall Analysis
- **Evidence requirements**: Must be 100% real, externally verifiable - no fabricated references
- **Rating scale**: A (Definitely True) through F (Definitely False)
- **Labels**: specious, extreme-right, weak, baseless, personal attack, emotional, defensive, progressive, woke, conservative, pandering, fallacious
