# Architecture of Trust: AI-Augmented Real Estate Valuation

## Summary

ArXiv paper (2508.02765) analyzing the mandatory 2026 UAD 3.6 implementation transforming residential property appraisal from narrative to structured machine-readable formats. Framework for AI augmentation addressing trust, fairness, and uncertainty quantification.

## Key Concepts

- **UAD 3.6 mandatory implementation** (2026) for structured appraisal data
- **Three-layer architecture**: physical acquisition, semantic understanding, cognitive reasoning
- **Institutional failures** documented: inter-appraiser variability, systematic biases
- **Human-AI collaboration** frameworks augmenting professional expertise
- **Uncertainty quantification** for algorithmic predictions
- Domain-specific evaluation beyond generic AI benchmarks
