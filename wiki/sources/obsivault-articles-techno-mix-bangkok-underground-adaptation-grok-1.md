# Techno Mix Bangkok Underground Adaptation - Grok 1

## Summary

Dark techno warehouse set version tailored for Bangkok underground conditions. Adapts from European/Berlin style to Bangkok's heat, fatigue, alcohol-affected crowds, and bass-heavy sound systems. Features BPM +0.5 compared to Europe, emphasizing physical groove over contemplative deep moments. Core artists: Garnier, PAS, Hood, Mills, Seth Troxler, KiNK. Six phases: Dark Install (123-124 BPM) → Techno Groove (124-125.5) → Pressure Continue (126-127) → Peak Unique (127-128) → Twist Funk Sale (126.5-125) → After Underground (123-124). Peak moment: Jeff Mills - The Bells.

## Key Concepts

- Bangkok-specific calibration: BPM +0.5, tighter mixes, less talk
- Public less patient than Berlin, sound systems bass-heavy
- Reduction of soul/melody, increased hypnotic repetition
- Garnier as anchor, Hood/PAS for pressure, Mills for peak
- Avoided: Lindstrøm, Theo Parrish, Sven Väth (too subtle/polished)
- Safety fallback: Robert Hood when uncertain
