# 2023 Investment Thesis - Deeptech and Crypto

## Summary

Rand Hindi's 2023 investment thesis covering deeptech and crypto. Deeptech focus areas: Next Generation Computing (quantum, photonics, RISC-V, chip unbundling), Frontier Health (diagnostics, psychedelics, longevity), and emerging areas (fusion, space, geoengineering). Crypto thesis: 4-phase cycle (build → bubble → crash → repeat), with focus on on-chain privacy (Zama/homomorphic encryption), decentralized identities (DID wallets, KYC tokens), blockchain gaming, and decentralized social networks. Key insight: "Whatever developers get excited about ends up driving the crypto market."

## Key Concepts

- Moore's Law end → quantum computing and photonics opportunity
- Hardware unbundling: dedicated chips for AI, ZK, FHE
- RISC-V open-source hardware revolution
- Psychedelics for mental health: precision molecule design
- Crypto cycle: build → bubble → crash → repeat
- Privacy on chain: homomorphic encryption for smart contracts
- DID: wallet-as-identity with ZK attestations
