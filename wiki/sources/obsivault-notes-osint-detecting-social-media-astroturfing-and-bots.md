# OSINT - Detecting Social Media Astroturfing and Bots

## Summary

Comprehensive guide to detecting social media bots and astroturfing using OSINT techniques. Covers bot types (customer service, social, informational), implications of artificial inflation and coordinated campaigns. Four detection methodologies: Behavioral Analysis (posting speed, hours, repetition), Metadata/Account Analysis (creation date, activity, handles), Network Analysis (bot-to-bot interactions), and Impression Analysis (impressions vs engagement ratio). Tools: Botometer, Hoaxy, CrowdTangle. Emphasizes digital literacy and ethical considerations for accurate identification.

## Key Concepts

- Astroturfing: artificial grassroots amplification
- Behavioral anomalies: superhuman posting speeds, inhuman hours
- Bot networks: coordinated inauthentic social circles
- Botometer, Hoaxy, CrowdTangle as detection tools
- Impression-to-engagement ratio discrepancies
- Digital literacy as societal defense
