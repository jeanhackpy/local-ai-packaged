## Résumé

Service premium de FazWaz pour vendre des propriétés en Thaïlande. Combine marketing multi-canal (10 portals), financement alternatif (rent-to-own, seller financing, gap loans), et accompagnement dédié. Couvre Bangkok, Phuket, Koh Samui, Pattaya, Hua Hin, Chiang Mai.

## Points clés

### Multi-channel syndication
- 10 major property portals
- ~10M impressions vs ~3M standard
- Syndication automatique

### Alternative financing (clé differentiation)
- **Rent-to-Own** : Loyer premium avec option d'achat
- **Seller Financing** : 30% down, 5 ans, 5% intérêt
- **Gap Loan** : Pour buyers avec 70%+ ready mais besoin bridge
- Impact : banques rejettent ~70% des buyers → huge underserved market

### Professional services
- Photography + video walkthrough
- Home valuation model (comparable sales, market trends, buyer behavior)
- Single point of contact
- Seller dashboard (real-time analytics)

### Pricing
- No extra cost (standard commission only)
- Exclusive listing required
