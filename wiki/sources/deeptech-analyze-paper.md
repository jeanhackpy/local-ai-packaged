# Analyze Paper - Research Paper Quality Evaluation

## Summary

A research paper analysis service evaluating scientific rigor and quality. Extracts authors, organizations, findings, study overview, and rates study design, sample size, confidence intervals, p-value, effect size, methodology transparency, and reproducibility. Final grade A-F based on novelty, rigor, empiricism.

## Key Concepts

- **Output sections**: Summary (16 words), Authors, Author Organizations, Findings (10 bullets, 16 words max), Study Overview (16-word bullets), Study Quality (design, sample, CI, p-value, effect size, consistence, methodology, reproducibility, data analysis), Conflicts of Interest, Researcher's Interpretation, Paper Quality (Novelty/Rigor/Empiricism 1-10 with charts)
- **Rating chart**: Known-to-Novel, Weak-to-Rigorous, Theoretical-to-Empirical
- **Final Score**: A-F grade with 15-word explanation
- **Tone**: Paul Graham's clear, approachable writing style

## Connections
