# Architecture of Trust for Real Estate AI

## Summary

Framework for AI-augmented real estate valuation focusing on trust architecture. Addresses UAD 3.6 compliance, structured data era implications, and human-AI collaboration model. Three-layer architecture: physical data acquisition, semantic understanding, cognitive reasoning.

## Key Concepts

- **Trust Architecture** for AI valuation systems
- **UAD 3.6** mandatory implementation (2026)
- **Structured Data Era** transformation from narrative to machine-readable
- **Human-AI Collaboration** model
- **Inter-appraiser variability** addressed
- **Algorithmic fairness** and uncertainty quantification

## Paper Details

- **arXiv ID**: 2508.02765
- **Authors**: Petteri Teikari, Mike Jarrell, Maryam Azh, Harri Pesola
- **Subjects**: cs.CY, cs.AI, cs.CV

## Key Insight

> "Successful transformation requires not merely technological sophistication but careful human-AI collaboration, creating systems that augment rather than replace professional expertise."
