## Agentic AI

Paradigme où l'AI ne se limite pas à générer du contenu mais **exécute des actions, orchestre des workflows, et automatise des processus** avec oversight humain.

### Différence avec GenAI

| GenAI | Agentic AI |
|-------|------------|
| Answer questions | Get things done |
| Produce content | Take actions |
| Work in isolation | Operate within workflows/systems |

### Patterns clés

- **Workflow automation** : Multi-step tasks (intake, triage, compliance)
- **Cross-system coordination** : Scheduling, routing, approvals, escalations
- **Decision support** : Rules + thresholds + interpretation
- **Human-in-the-loop** : Trust mais pas full autonomy

### Best practices (Deloitte lessons)

1. Trust is non-negotiable
2. Embed in workflows (amplifies, doesn't fix broken processes)
3. Understand human role (elevate, not replace)
4. Build enterprise capability, not one-off projects
5. Start with 1-3 anchor use cases
