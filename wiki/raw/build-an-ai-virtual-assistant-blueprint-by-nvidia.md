## Summary

This NVIDIA blueprint provides a foundation for building AI-powered virtual assistants designed for customer service operations. It enables automated, contextually aware responses to common inquiries while improving overall user satisfaction through intelligent automation.

## Points Clés

- **RAG-based Architecture**: Utilizes retrieval-augmented generation for accurate, context-aware responses
- **Nemo Text Retriever E5**: For embedding-based semantic search
- **NVIDIA Retrieval QA Mistral 4B**: Reranking model for improved answer quality
- **Customer Data Integration**: Supports personalized responses based on purchase history and customer context
- **Multi-industry Applicability**: Adaptable across retail, healthcare, telecom, and other service sectors
