## Analyze Physical State of the Villa

### Interior

- **Structures and foundations:** cracks, signs of dampness, subsidence.
- **Equipment:** air conditioning, swimming pool, household appliances, etc.: operating condition, obsolescence.
- **Electrical and plumbing systems:** leaks, malfunctions, safety standards.

### Exterior

- **Walls, roofs, and gutters:** cracks, water infiltration, signs of wear.
- **Garden and green spaces:** maintenance, landscaping, condition of plants and trees.
- **Swimming pool:** structure, coating, filtration and maintenance system.

*Tips:*
- Do not hesitate to call on a building expert for a thorough inspection.
- Request invoices and documents attesting to the maintenance and repairs carried out.
- Pay attention to details and note all points to be renovated or monitored.
