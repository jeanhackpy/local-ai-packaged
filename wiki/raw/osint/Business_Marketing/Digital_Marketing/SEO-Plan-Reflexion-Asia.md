### SEO Plan for reflexion.asia

#### 1. Initial SEO Audit
- **Technical Analysis**:
  - Check for crawl errors, site speed, and mobile compatibility.
  - Ensure robots.txt and XML sitemap are correctly configured.
- **Content Analysis**:
  - Identify top-performing pages and those needing improvement.
  - Evaluate the quality and relevance of existing content.

#### 2. On-Page SEO Optimization
- **Keyword Research**:
  - Identify relevant keywords for your real estate niche in Thailand.
  - Use tools like Google Keyword Planner, SEMrush, or Ahrefs.
- **Tag Optimization**:
  - Optimize titles, meta descriptions, and URLs to include relevant keywords.
  - Use H1, H2, H3 tags correctly to structure content.
- **Quality Content**:
  - Write detailed and engaging descriptions for each property.
  - Regularly add useful and informative content via a blog (articles on real estate, tips, local guides, etc.).
- **Images and Multimedia**:
  - Use high-quality images with optimized alt tags.
  - Integrate videos when relevant (property tours, client testimonials, etc.).

#### 3. Off-Page SEO Optimization
- **Backlinking**:
  - Develop a backlinking strategy by obtaining links from relevant real estate sites, blogs, and quality directories.
  - Propose guest articles on niche sites.
  - Participate in online forums and discussions related to real estate.
- **Social Networks**:
  - Maintain an active presence on social networks to generate traffic and links to your site.
  - Regularly share blog content and property updates.
  
#### 4. Blog and Content Marketing
- **Content Strategy**:
  - Regularly publish blog articles optimized for identified keywords.
  - Create varied content: articles, guides, case studies, videos, infographics.
- **Editorial Calendar**:
  - Establish an editorial calendar to ensure regular publications.
  - Analyze the performance of each article to adjust the strategy accordingly.

#### 5. Monitoring and Analysis
- **Analytics Tools**:
  - Use Google Analytics, Google Search Console, and other SEO tools to track performance.
  - Analyze data to identify performing pages and opportunities for improvement.
- **Regular Reports**:
  - Prepare monthly SEO reports to track progress and adjust strategies.
