### Paid Advertising
- **Google Ads:** Run targeted Google Ads campaigns to appear in search results for your chosen keywords.
- **Social Media Ads:** Use Facebook, LinkedIn, or Instagram ads to reach your target audience with tailored content.
- **Retargeting:** Implement retargeting campaigns to reach users who have previously visited your site but didn’t convert.
