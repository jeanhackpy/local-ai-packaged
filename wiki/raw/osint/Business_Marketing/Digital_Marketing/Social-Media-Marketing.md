### Social Media Marketing
- **Platform Selection:** Choose the most relevant social media platforms for your target audience (e.g., LinkedIn for B2B, Facebook, and Instagram for a broader audience).
- **Regular Posting:** Maintain a consistent posting schedule with a mix of content types (articles, infographics, videos, polls).
- **Engagement:** Actively engage with your audience by responding to comments, joining industry groups, and participating in discussions.
