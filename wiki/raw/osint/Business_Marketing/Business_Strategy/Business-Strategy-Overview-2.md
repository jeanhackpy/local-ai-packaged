### Business Strategy:

1. **Market Trends:**
   - Growing interest in augmented reality for immersive home experiences.
   - Increasing demand for unique and personalized home decor solutions.

2. **Competitor Analysis:**
   - Identify competitors in the AR home decor market.
   - Analyze their product features, pricing strategies, and customer feedback.
   - Emphasize on the unique aspects of your product.

3. **SWOT Analysis:**
   - **Strengths:** Cutting-edge AR technology, personalized art experiences.
   - **Weaknesses:** Potential niche market, initial product understanding.
   - **Opportunities:** Expanding AR home decor market, growing interest in NFTs.
   - **Threats:** Rapid technological advancements, market saturation.

4. **Unique Selling Propositions (USPs):**
   - Enable users to integrate famous artworks or their creations seamlessly into their home.
   - Provide a platform for using AR markers on walls for interactive art experiences.
   - Offer compatibility with NFTs, allowing users to display digital assets as real objects.
