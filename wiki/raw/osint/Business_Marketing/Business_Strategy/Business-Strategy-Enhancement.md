### Business Strategy Enhancement:

1. **Market Trends:**
   - Recognize the growing interest in multi-sensory experiences.
   - Identify the demand for innovative audio-visual products in the tech market.

2. **Competitor Analysis:**
   - Evaluate competitors offering AR and audio experiences.
   - Analyze their audio technology, sound libraries, and user feedback.
   - Emphasize on the integration of sound creation for a holistic experience.

3. **SWOT Analysis:**
   - **Strengths:** Cutting-edge AR technology, personalized 3D art experiences, integrated sound creation.
   - **Weaknesses:** Potential user learning curve, initial market understanding.
   - **Opportunities:** Tapping into the emerging market of multi-sensory AR experiences.
   - **Threats:** Rapid technological advancements, market competition.

4. **Unique Selling Propositions (USPs):**
   - Seamless integration of sound creation with AR visual experiences.
   - Offer a library of diverse sounds to complement various art and decor scenarios.
   - Provide tools for users to create and customize soundscapes for their surroundings.
