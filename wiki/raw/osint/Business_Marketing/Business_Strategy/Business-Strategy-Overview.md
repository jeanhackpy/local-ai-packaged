### Business Strategy:

1. **Market Trends:**
   - AR technology is gaining popularity, especially in the tech-savvy market.
   - Lightweight and connected wearables are sought after for convenience.

2. **Competitor Analysis:**
   - Identify key competitors in the AR eyewear market.
   - Analyze their product features, pricing strategies, and customer reviews.
   - Differentiate your product based on unique features and benefits.

3. **SWOT Analysis:**
   - **Strengths:** Innovative AR technology, lightweight design.
   - **Weaknesses:** Initial market entry, potential competition.
   - **Opportunities:** Growing AR market, increasing demand for wearables.
   - **Threats:** Rapid technological advancements, potential regulatory challenges.

4. **Unique Selling Propositions (USPs):**
   - Highlight the lightweight design for enhanced comfort.
   - Emphasize the advanced AR technology for an immersive experience.
   - Position the product as a blend of style and functionality.
