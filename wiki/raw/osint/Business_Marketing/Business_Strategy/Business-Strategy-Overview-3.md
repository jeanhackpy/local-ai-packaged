### Business Strategy:

#### Market Trends and Analysis:
1. **Research Popular Products:** Identify trending products in the French market by using tools like Jungle Scout or Helium 10. Consider products aligned with your passions and hobbies.
2. **Competitor Analysis:** Analyze successful competitors on Amazon France. Understand their product offerings, pricing strategies, and customer reviews.

#### SWOT Analysis:
- **Strengths:**
  - Your diverse interests can help you choose a unique and appealing product.
  - Understanding of blockchain and web3 can be advantageous for innovative marketing.

- **Weaknesses:**
  - Lack of experience in Amazon FBA – consider hiring or partnering with someone experienced.
  - Initial challenges in balancing diverse interests within the product and brand.

- **Opportunities:**
  - Growing interest in smart technologies can be tapped into for product development.
  - Utilize blockchain and web3 in marketing strategies for transparency and trust.

- **Threats:**
  - Intense competition in the Amazon marketplace.
  - Regulatory challenges in certain product categories.

#### Unique Selling Propositions (USPs):
- **Sustainability:** Emphasize eco-friendly packaging or sustainable sourcing, aligning with your interest in permaculture.
- **Smart Technology Integration:** If applicable, incorporate IoT or other smart technologies into your product.
