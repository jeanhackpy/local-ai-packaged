### Business Development Plan for AiroKoi in Bangkok, Thailand

#### 1. Market Analysis:
- **E-commerce Landscape:** Analyze the local e-commerce market, including competitors, consumer behavior, and emerging trends.
- **Real Estate Integration:** Explore opportunities to integrate OSINT (Open-Source Intelligence) data into real estate services.
- **Blockchain Applications:** Research potential blockchain applications in e-commerce and real estate sectors.

#### 2. Partnerships & Collaborations:
- **Real Estate Agencies:** Forge partnerships with local real estate agencies to leverage OSINT for property evaluation.
- **Blockchain Developers:** Collaborate with blockchain developers to explore innovative applications in the e-commerce domain.

#### 3. Product Development:
- **AI-Powered Marketplace:** Develop an AI-driven e-commerce platform offering personalized product recommendations.
- **Real Estate Analytics Tool:** Create a tool integrating OSINT data for property evaluations and market analysis.
- **Blockchain Integration:** Implement blockchain technology for secure transactions and data management.

#### 4. Financial Strategy:
- **Funding Sources:** Explore venture capital, angel investors, or grants focused on technology-driven ventures.
- **Revenue Models:** Consider subscription-based services for real estate analytics and commissions from e-commerce sales.
