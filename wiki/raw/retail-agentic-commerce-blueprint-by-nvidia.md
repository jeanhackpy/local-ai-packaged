## Summary

A reference implementation enabling AI agents to autonomously discover products, negotiate promotions, complete secure checkouts, and deliver personalized post-purchase experiences while maintaining merchant control over pricing, payments, and compliance. Implements dual protocols (ACP/UCP) for standardized agent-to-merchant communication.

## Points Clés

- **Dual Protocol Support**: ACP (Agentic Commerce Protocol) + UCP (Universal Commerce Protocol)
- **NeMo Agent Toolkit**: Multi-agent orchestration for complex shopping workflows
- **NVIDIA Nemotron LLM**: Dynamic pricing and promotion strategy selection
- **ARAG Pipeline**: Agentic RAG with specialized agents for cross-sell recommendations
- **NV-EmbedQA-E5**: Semantic product embeddings with Milvus vector search
- **Apps SDK + MCP**: Standardized integration for merchant-owned widgets
- **Multilingual Post-Purchase**: AI-generated shipping updates in configurable brand tones
