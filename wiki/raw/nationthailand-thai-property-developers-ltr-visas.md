# Thai Property Developers Bundle Condos with Long-Stay Visas

## Source Document

Article Nation Thailand sur le shift stratégique des développeurs thaïs : packaging LTR visa avec achat condo. Compétition qui passe de "location/price/design" à "after-sales services" niveau international.

- [[Condo bundle offers]] — Visa packages with property purchases

## Market Data

- **Sansiri foreign sales target**: >6.4 billion baht
- **LTR purchase threshold**: 3 million baht (upper-middle foreign investors)
- **ORN visa structure**: 15 months first year, 12 months following years

## Developer Strategies

### Sansiri
- Launching XT 10 Ekkamai and LOVE Charoennakhon
- Target markets: China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Myanmar, Europe, Middle East
- Foreign sales target: >6.4B baht

### AP Thailand
- Partnering with Thai Longstay for The Address, Rhythm, Life, Aspire
- Properties near mass transit
- Bundle LTR visa packages

### Ornsirin
- Targeting Chiang Mai and Phuket markets

## Target Demographics

- Asian investors seeking portfolio diversification
- Digital nomads
- Retirees
- Second-home buyers in tourism cities

## Key Trend

"Competition is no longer confined to the domestic market, but is instead about competing for global investors."

Developers shifting from:
- **Seller** → **Full-scale service provider**
- Asset sale → Living ecosystem building

## Risks

- Government policy continuity
- Foreign ownership quota restrictions
- Balance genuine demand vs speculative buying
