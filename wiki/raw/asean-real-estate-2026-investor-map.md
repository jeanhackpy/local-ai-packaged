## Thailand Data

- **Gross yield**: 6.4% average
- **Phuket villas**: 8–15% gross yield
- **Safety Index**: 63.4 (3rd safest in SEA)
- **LTR Visa**: 10-year, 0% tax on foreign-sourced income
- **Recommendation**: Primary allocation — highest yields with improving legal framework

## Indonesia (Bali) Data

- **Villa yields**: 8–15% gross
- **Canggu**: 9% commonly cited
- **International visitors**: 7.1M in 2025 (record)
- **Safety Index**: 53.6
- **Transparency**: 40th (JLL)
- **Recommendation**: Primary allocation

## Vietnam Data

- **HCMC formal yield**: 3.7% gross
- **Airbnb yields**: 12.3%
- **Price growth**: +22.3% YoY (Q3 2024)
- **New Land Law 2025**: Improved investor protections
- **Investment Law 2026**: Further framework improvements
- **Recommendation**: Watch list (2-3 year horizon) — fastest growing economy in SEA

## Malaysia Data

- **Average yield**: 5.19%
- **KL city-fringe**: 6.1%
- **JB-Singapore RTS**: Driving demand
- **MM2H program**: Silver/Gold/Platinum tiers
- **Recommendation**: Secondary allocation — stronger legal protections

## Philippines Data

- **National avg gross yield**: 7.48%
- **Net yield**: 5.86%
- **Davao townhouses**: 8.57% gross (top)
- **Safety Index**: 56.5
- **Transparency**: 45th (JLL)
- **Recommendation**: Secondary allocation — freehold condo exposure

## Singapore Data

- **Gross yield**: 3.13%
- **Net yield**: 1.5–2%
- **ABSD foreign buyers**: 60%
- **Safety Index**: 77.5 (safest in SEA)
- **Recommendation**: Wealth preservation only — capital safety, yield-compressed

## Strategic Recommendations

1. **Primary allocation**: Bali or Phuket — highest yields (8–15%) with improving legal frameworks and strong tourism demand
2. **Secondary allocation**: Malaysia or Philippines — freehold condo exposure with stronger legal protections
3. **Wealth preservation**: Singapore — capital safety and liquidity unmatched, but yield-compressed
4. **Watch list (2–3 year horizon)**: Vietnam — fastest-growing economy in SEA with improving regulatory framework
